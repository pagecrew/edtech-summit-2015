Bio

Gugulethu Radebe is currently an intern teacher at Inanda Seminary, an all-girls’ boarding school in Durban. She is passionate about education and the effect it has on the social roles that students undertake at later stages in their lives.She is a public speaking coach and a facilitator for BOLD, a girls empowerment group that is part of a global network. Coupled with her love for education and women’s empowerment, she is also a great believer in sustainable and eco-friendly use of technology. She is currently studying for a Bachelor in Education through the University of South Africa (UNISA) majoring in English and History. She is the Media and Communications Director for EdTech Africa 2015 and a presenter on the workshop team.
