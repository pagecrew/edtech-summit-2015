Bio

Questions about effective teaching and learning have been at the core of Esther O. Ohito’s work for the last dozen years. Esther has engaged with this work in various roles—first in her capacity as a National Board Certified English Language Arts middle school teacher in Chicago, Illinois, then as a teacher educator for pre-service and beginning teachers with The University of Chicago and Teachers College, Columbia University, and now as a doctoral fellow with a focus on curriculum studies and teacher education also at Teachers College, Columbia University. Esther is excited by the opportunity to learn from others while contributing as an individual and as part of a collective towards the attainment of a just global society; one that enables teachers to support students—particularly those most marginalized—in learning and leading healthy lives in and beyond classrooms and schools.
