Bio

I’m a tech man of action!

I live in Cape Town, and I have been working at Leap Science and Maths Schools for over three years as an IT expert. Configuring LDAP, Squid, and Samba, plus keeping at least twenty end users’ technology alive makes me happy. I love learning programming languages, web design, and new tools. I enjoy transferring knowledge almost as much as learning new stuff.

I’m a quiet guy, but love helping people solve problems. My passion for sharing knowledge with educators is a dream come true.
