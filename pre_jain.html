Bio

Rupal is a Learning Designer and believer in the infinite possibility of the human spirit. For the past nine years Rupal has worked as an award-winning educator, curriculum designer, and teacher education specialist, both domestically and abroad. I believe the greatest power for educational change rests in the hands of our teachers. It is my life's work to empower educators with the tools and resources they need to design high quality learning environments for their students. I see technology as a great partner in bringing students environments primed for learning. Through the thoughtful use of technology, educators can enable all learners to seek deep questions, collaborate purposefully with one another, and bring their ideas to life. I am honored to have the opportunity to share with and learn from the participants of this year's EdTech Summit Africa as we all work together to uncover the best ways to leverage technology to raise the quality of education for all students.
